Nutrition Tips

Eat the Rainbow

Include a variety of colorful fruits and vegetables in your diet to ensure you're getting a wide range of nutrients. Different colors provide different health benefits.

  • Red foods support heart health
  • Orange foods boost immunity
  • Green foods aid digestion
Hydration Matters

Water supports digestion, clear skin, and overall energy. Small, consistent habits can make a big difference in your hydration.

  • Start with 1-2 glasses in the morning
  • Drink 8-10 glasses daily
  • Eat water-rich foods like cucumbers
Mindful Eating

Slow down and pay attention to your food. Mindful eating can help with digestion and prevent overeating.

  • Chew each bite 20-30 times
  • Eliminate distractions during meals
  • Listen to your body's hunger cues
